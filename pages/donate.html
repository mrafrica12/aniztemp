Support Aniz, Inc.

Every dollar you give funds free HIV testing, mental health counseling, youth programs, and holistic health services for people who need them most. Your generosity makes a direct difference in Atlanta's most underserved communities.

Your Gift Provides

  • Free and confidential HIV & STI testing
  • Mental health counseling for individuals and families
  • Youth programming and holiday toy giveaways
  • Peer support groups for people living with HIV
  • Harm reduction supplies and substance use support
  • Community health fairs and outreach events

Aniz, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
