Community Outreach

ANIZ meets people where they are. Our outreach program reaches underserved populations throughout Atlanta with critical support services — bringing health resources directly into the community.

What Our Outreach Provides

  • Strategic outreach targeting at-risk populations
  • HIV and STI testing in the community
  • Free Hepatitis C screening
  • Mental health support and referrals
  • Substance use counseling and referrals
  • Harm reduction education and supplies
  • Sterile syringe distribution
  • Specialized support for people living with HIV
  • Program enrollment assistance

Get Connected

Our outreach team attends community events, health fairs, and neighborhood gatherings across Atlanta. Check our events page or follow us on social media to find us in your neighborhood.

If you would like ANIZ to bring outreach services to your organization or event, contact us below.
