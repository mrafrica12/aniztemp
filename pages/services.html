Services

At Aniz, Inc. we provide a wide range of holistic health services to support individuals and communities. From HIV prevention and care to mental health support and substance use counseling — we are here to help.

Mental Health Counseling

Individual, group, and family counseling with licensed therapists, coping strategies, and compassionate ongoing support.

Learn more

HIV/STI Testing

Free, confidential, rapid HIV and STI testing — including free Hepatitis C screening — with counseling and linkage to care.

Learn more

PrEP Care

The E4U PrEP program helps HIV-negative clients understand eligibility and start a daily pill that reduces HIV risk by up to 99%. Walk-ins welcome.

Learn more

Community Outreach

Targeted outreach connecting underserved communities to HIV testing, mental health, substance use, harm reduction, sterile syringes, and enrollment support.

Learn more

Ryan White Services

Medical and support services for people living with HIV — counseling, peer support, transportation referrals, and care coordination.

Learn more

Substance Use Services

The F.R.E.E. program offers evidence-based individual and group counseling, education, peer support, and holistic harm reduction.

Learn more

Peer Support Groups

Safe, facilitated groups for people living with HIV, women's wellness, and mental health/substance use support. Multiple sessions weekly.

Learn more

Health Education

Tailored programs addressing mental health, substance use, STI prevention, and sexual health to empower individuals with knowledge and skills.

Learn more

West Cobb Pharmacy

Pharmacy services supporting client medication access and adherence as part of ANIZ's holistic care coordination approach.

Learn more
