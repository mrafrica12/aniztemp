Mental Health Counseling

Your mental health matters. We offer a range of services to help stabilize individuals and provide ongoing support — including individual therapy, group therapy, substance abuse treatment, and peer support. Our interdisciplinary team meets clients where they are.

What We Offer

  • Individual therapy
  • Group therapy
  • Family counseling
  • Substance abuse treatment
  • Peer support services
  • Case management
  • Community outreach and referrals

Our team includes licensed therapists, counselors, peer support specialists, case managers, and outreach staff — all committed to supporting your emotional wellness journey.
