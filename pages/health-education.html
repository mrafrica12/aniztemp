Health Education

Knowledge is power. ANIZ provides tailored health education programs that equip individuals, families, and communities with the tools to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.

Education Programs

Our programs are designed for real people — accessible, culturally relevant, and delivered with compassion.

HIV/AIDS Awareness & Prevention

Comprehensive education on HIV transmission, prevention, testing, treatment, and reducing stigma in our communities. Includes information about PrEP, U=U (Undetectable = Untransmittable), and linkage to care.

STI Prevention Education

Information on STI transmission, prevention, symptoms, and the importance of routine testing — delivered in a judgment-free, supportive environment.

Mental Health Literacy

Programs that help individuals recognize signs of mental health challenges, reduce stigma around seeking help, and connect with counseling and peer support resources.

Substance Use Prevention

Education for youth, adults, and families on the risks of substance use, harm reduction strategies, and pathways to support and recovery through our F.R.E.E. program.

Sexual Health Education

Age-appropriate, inclusive sexual health education addressing healthy relationships, consent, contraception, and disease prevention.

Community Workshops & Health Fairs

ANIZ facilitates educational workshops at health fairs, community events, churches, schools, and partner organizations across the Atlanta area.

Request an Educational Program

We partner with schools, faith communities, employers, and community organizations to bring health education directly to your audience. To request a workshop or presentation, complete the form or call us at (404) 521-2410.
