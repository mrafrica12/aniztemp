Ryan White Services

The Ryan White HIV Program stands as a beacon of hope and support for individuals affected by HIV/AIDS in our communities. Aniz, Inc. provides a comprehensive range of services to help clients manage their health and improve their quality of life.

Services We Provide

  • Individual mental health counseling
  • Group mental health counseling
  • Individual substance use counseling
  • Group substance use counseling
  • Individual and group peer support
  • Medical transportation assistance
  • Referral to medical and support services
  • Care coordination and case management

Our team works alongside each client to develop a personalized care plan addressing their health, emotional, and social needs.
