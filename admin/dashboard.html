Content Management

Use these tools to update the site's frequently-changed content without editing HTML files.

📢

Announcement Banner

Edit the rotating announcements that appear at the top of every page. Add new topics, change the AIDS Walk message, or disable individual announcements.
📅

Events

Add upcoming events with date, time, location, description, and image. Reorder or remove past events. Changes appear live on the public Events page.
👥

Our Team

Add new staff members, update titles, change photos, or remove staff who have left. Changes appear live on the About / Our Team section.
🖼

Gallery

Add new event photos, remove outdated images, and reorder the gallery grid. Changes appear live on the public Gallery page.
